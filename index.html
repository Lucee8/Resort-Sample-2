Our Story

Where Sea Meets
Serenity

Nestled on the pristine shores of Devbag, The Windmill Resorts is a coastal haven that perfectly marries serenity with luxury — embraced by the Arabian Sea on one side and the tranquil Karli River on the other.

01

Panoramic Ocean Views

Almost every room opens to sweeping panoramas — wake up to the enchanting sight of waves meeting the shore.

02

Infinity Pool & Sun Deck

Our rejuvenating infinity pool with ocean views provides the perfect backdrop for effortless relaxation.

03

Beachfront Celebrations

Romantic beachfront weddings, engagements, and corporate events — we set the stage for the unforgettable.

04

Coastal Adventure

Scuba diving, beach cycling, water sports, and coastal exploration — the adventure begins at your doorstep.
